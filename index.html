Core Problem: Why Netflix Underperforms

Content Gap

Only 4% Arabic titles on Netflix KSA vs Shahid's 51% local productions → ~3x more relevant content

Cultural Timing Gap

Netflix has no Ramadan programming, while Shahid dominates the annual peak viewing period

Discovery Gap

Saudi creators drive massive conversions → Netflix barely uses influencer loops

Pricing & Distribution Gap

Weak STC/Mobily/Zain bundling historically; Shahid + StarzPlay leverage telcos effectively

5 Key Plays to Win Saudi

1
Invest in Arabic Originals
  • Produce 5–10 high-quality Arabic series per year
  • Leverage top Saudi creators for local appeal
  • Launch Ramadan special series for annual peaks
2
Bundling & Partnerships
  • Exclusive STC/Mobily/Zain partnerships
  • Offer special promos to new subscribers
  • Integrate Netflix with local OTT apps for visibility
3
Local Creator Campaigns
  • Top Saudi TikTok/YouTube influencers for series promotion
  • Creator co-productions to increase engagement
  • Use social-first short content to drive subscriptions
4
Targeted Discovery & UX
  • Highlight Arabic content on homepage
  • Dynamic recommendations for Ramadan & local holidays
  • Push notifications for local premieres
5
Marketing Localization
  • Arabic-language campaigns across TV, social, and OOH
  • Highlight cultural relevance and Saudi talent
  • Measure engagement & iterate weekly
